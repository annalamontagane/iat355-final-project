SECTION 5: DESIGN PRINCIPLES
05 · Design Principles
Hierarchy

Information is organized into clear levels: KPI cards at the top, supporting charts below, detail tables at the bottom. Bold weight signals what matters most.

Color as Signal

Red (--mc-red) is reserved for high-priority alerts. Orange (--mc-orange) marks interactive elements and medium-severity states. Yellow for low priority.

Contrast

Dark surfaces (#0A0A0A to #1E1E1E) maximize chart legibility. White text on dark backgrounds ensures WCAG AA compliance across key text elements.

Progressive Disclosure

Three-level drill-down (Overview → Deep Dive → Rule Detail) exposes complexity only when the analyst needs it. WIP banners communicate honest completion state.
